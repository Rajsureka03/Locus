Logos

Memphis Style Logo

This year's main Twilight logo is designed using Memphis style. This is to evoke a sense of nostalgia as it is reminiscent of the 1980's.

Circular Logo

The circular logo was designed to be used in instances where a circular frame works better than a square, like social media profile pictures.

AOC Michelob Logo

This white logo was designed as a simpler, more indentifiable alternative to the main logo. Because Athens Orthopedic Clinic and Michelob are such large sponsors, it is imperative that they are showcased on the logos.

Red AOC Michelob Logo

This logo serves the same purpose as the white logo but is situated on a Raspberry Wine backdrop. This serves as an alternative to the white logo.
